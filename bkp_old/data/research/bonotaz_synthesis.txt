**Reconstructed Recipe (Vegan Bonotaz - A Starting Point)**

This is a conceptual recipe, based on available research and assumptions. Detailed family oral histories could greatly refine this approach.

**Ingredients:**

*   2 tbsp olive oil
*   1 large onion, chopped
*   4 cloves garlic, minced
*   1 red bell pepper, chopped
*   1 yellow bell pepper, chopped
*   1 eggplant, diced
*   1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
*   1 cup cooked chickpeas (or butter beans or lentils)
*   2 tbsp harissa paste (adjust to taste)
*   2 tsp cumin
*   2 tsp coriander
*   1 tsp smoked paprika
*   4 cups vegetable broth
*   1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro and parsley
*   Salt and pepper to taste
*   Optional: 1 preserved lemon, finely chopped
*   Optional: Toasted almonds or pine nuts for garnish

**Instructions:**

1.  **Sauté Aromatics:** Heat olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat. Add onion and cook until softened, about 5-7 minutes. Add garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
2.  **Add Vegetables:** Add bell peppers and eggplant and cook until slightly softened, about 5-7 minutes.
3.  **Add Spices and Tomato:** Stir in harissa, cumin, coriander, and smoked paprika. Cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly. Add crushed tomatoes and cook for another 5 minutes.
4.  **Add Legumes and Broth:** Add chickpeas and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for at least 1 hour, or preferably longer (overnight in a slow cooker is ideal).
5.  **Adjust Seasoning:** Taste and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper as needed.
6.  **Add Herbs and Garnish:** Stir in fresh cilantro and parsley. If using, stir in preserved lemon.
7.  **Serve:** Serve warm, garnished with toasted almonds or pine nuts (if desired). Serve with pita bread or laffa for dipping.
